Welcome to TNF Wellness Clinic

TNF Wellness Clinic was created with the vision of bringing health, wellness and community together under one roof.

Our mission is to help people feel stronger, healthier and more connected within themselves, both physically and mentally.

What We Offer

Neuromuscular Therapy

Consultation, assessment and treatment to support pain relief, recovery and better movement.

Massage Therapy

Tailored 30, 60 and 90 minute treatments to help you relax, recover and reset.

Fitness & Wellness

Pay-as-you-go fitness classes, wellbeing support and a welcoming community space.

More Than Just A Clinic

TNF Wellness is a welcoming wellness hub where like-minded professionals come together to offer support, care and services that truly benefit the individual.

From fitness and recovery to massage, healing, wellbeing services and classes, the goal is to create one space where people can access everything they need to support their journey.
