TNF Wellness Clinic is committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it and your rights regarding your personal data when you use our website or contact us.

Information We Collect

If you contact us through our website, by email or by phone, we may collect personal information including:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number, where provided
  • The information included in your enquiry

We only collect information that you choose to provide to us.

How We Use Your Information

Any personal information you provide may be used to:

  • Respond to your enquiry
  • Arrange appointments or classes
  • Answer questions about our services
  • Provide customer support
  • Meet our legal obligations where required

We do not sell, rent or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Contact Forms

If you contact us using the contact form on this website, the information you submit will only be used to respond to your enquiry and provide the services you have requested.

Cookies

This website only uses cookies that are necessary for the website to function correctly.

If analytics or additional services are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Third-Party Services

This website may contain links to third-party websites or services, including:

  • Google Maps
  • Facebook
  • Instagram
  • WhatsApp

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them before providing any personal information.

How Long We Keep Your Information

Personal information is only retained for as long as necessary to respond to your enquiry, provide our services or meet legal obligations.

Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information where appropriate
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our services or legal requirements. Any updates will be published on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your personal information is handled, please contact us.
