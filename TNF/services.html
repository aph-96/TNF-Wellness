Signature Treatment

Neuromuscular Therapy Consultation & Treatment

This treatment is ideal for anyone dealing with injury, chronic pain, tightness, restricted movement or ongoing muscular discomfort.

Each session begins with a consultation and assessment to understand the root cause of your pain and create a treatment plan tailored specifically to you.

Your session may include:

  • Neuromuscular massage therapy
  • Deep tissue techniques
  • Cupping therapy
  • Dry needling

This treatment is designed to help reduce pain, improve mobility, release muscular tension and support recovery so you can move and feel better in everyday life.
